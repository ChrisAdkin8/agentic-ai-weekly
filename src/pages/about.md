**Agentic AI & LLM Weekly** is a curated digest of the most important developments in agentic AI, large language models, AI security, and the infrastructure underneath — published every week.

Coverage spans 11 source categories: community discussion, research papers, technical engineering blogs, analyst and VC reports, AI security, product news, regulatory policy, agent-era frameworks, open-source infrastructure, hardware and compute, and model evaluations.

## What you'll find here

Each issue contains:

- **Editor's Picks** — the top 3 stories of the week with a connecting theme
- **Community Pulse** — what practitioners are actually talking about on Reddit, Hacker News, X, and LinkedIn
- **Research Highlights** — papers from arXiv, alignment labs, and academic groups worth your time
- **Engineering & Technical Blogs** — what builders are shipping, from major labs to infra companies
- **AI Security & Safety** — threats, vulnerabilities, frameworks, and defences (OWASP, MITRE, NIST, CISA, Lakera, HiddenLayer, Embrace the Red)
- **Regulatory & Policy** — laws, rulings, and compliance moves shaping AI deployment
- **Agent Era & Technical Workflows** — patterns and architectures for production agentic systems
- **Model Evaluations** — how models are being measured and compared
- And more — hardware watch, open source, product news, analyst reports

## How it's made

Issues are curated and written by [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/claude-code) using the open-source [newsletter-ai](https://github.com/ChrisAdkin8/newsletter-ai-skill) skill — a Claude Code skill that searches across 100+ sources, filters for signal over noise, and produces a structured digest for technical practitioners.
